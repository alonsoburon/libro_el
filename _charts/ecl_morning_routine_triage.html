Framework 19
Morning routine triage
Four items, fixed order, every morning. Everything else is drill-down.
1
How many tables failed overnight
Zero = move on. Non-zero = which ones, since when.
2
Which tables are stale beyond their SLA
Ran but didn't finish in time, or loaded but data is old.
3
Any row count anomalies
Spikes, drops, reconciliation deltas above threshold.
4
Cost per day
Trending up? New table? Runaway query? Investigate.
Principle
If the aggregate doesn't trigger curiosity, the individual tables are fine. Drill down only when something looks off.
