Framework 14
Scheduling maturity
Predictable progression as pipeline count grows. Each stage is correct at its scale.
1
Single cron
few tables
Everything runs together. Simple, works when you have a dozen tables and a daily window. Falls apart past ~4 hours or when intraday is needed.
Window exceeded or intraday needed
2
Weight-based groups
dozens of tables
Tables split by size or duration across time slots. Better throughput, but groupings don't map to business needs.
Business freshness SLAs diverge across consumers
3
Consumer-driven groups
hundreds of tables
Tables grouped by downstream report/dashboard, scheduled to meet that consumer's freshness target. The destination.
DESTINATION
Warning
Don't over-engineer stage 3 when you have three dashboards.
