Framework 09
Hot / warm / cold freshness
Not every row needs the same refresh cadence
Hot
INTRADAY
Tolerates imprecision
Actively changing data: today's orders, open invoices, recent events.
Method: incremental when needed, FR when table is small enough.
overlap: lag window
Warm
DAILY
Catches what hot misses
Current month/quarter. Late cursor updates, backdated transactions, silent changes.
Method: FR of warm window, or wide-lag incremental.
overlap: lag window
Cold
WEEKLY / MONTHLY
The cleanup pass
Historical data: prior years, closed fiscal periods. Resets accumulated drift.
Method: full replace.
Key insight
Tier = cadence. Method = separate decision. A small table can full-replace even at hot frequency.
Lag window
7 days for clean systems. 30 for messy ERPs. Start at 7, widen if cold catches rows warm missed.
