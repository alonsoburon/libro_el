Purity vs freshness
Every pipeline decision takes a position on this spectrum
Purity MAX →
← MAX Freshness
1
Full replace every run
Purity max · Freshness low
2
Scoped full replace
Purity high · Freshness medium
3
Stateless window extraction
Purity medium · Freshness medium-high
4
Cursor-based incremental
Purity medium · Freshness high
5
High-frequency incremental
Purity variable · Freshness max -- purity debt accumulates
Decision chain
Full scan fits window? → Reliable cursor? → Hard deletes? → Freshness SLA?
Each "no" pushes right -- away from purity, toward freshness
